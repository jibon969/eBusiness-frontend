All About Peptides

Peptides are essential building blocks of the skin, playing a critical role in collagen production and skin health. They are short chains of amino acids that signal your body to produce collagen, improving skin elasticity and texture.

Benefits of Peptides

Peptides help smooth wrinkles, increase skin firmness, and boost hydration. Regular use can lead to youthful and plump skin.

COSRX Peptide Product Recommendation

The COSRX Advanced Snail Peptide Eye Cream is formulated with snail secretion and peptides to nourish the delicate eye area.

All About Niacinamide

Niacinamide, also known as Vitamin B3, is one of the most versatile skincare ingredients. It is known for its ability to minimize pores, regulate oil production, and improve the skin barrier function.

Benefits of Niacinamide

Niacinamide can help reduce the appearance of blemishes and redness, even out skin tone, and smooth fine lines and wrinkles.

COSRX Niacinamide Product Recommendation

The COSRX Oil-Free Ultra-Moisturizing Lotion with Birch Sap contains niacinamide to help calm and hydrate the skin while reducing irritation.

All About Snail Secretion (Mucin)

Snail secretion filtrate, commonly referred to as snail mucin, is a powerhouse ingredient known for its skin-repairing properties. It is packed with nutrients like hyaluronic acid, glycoprotein, and proteoglycans.

Benefits of Snail Mucin

Snail mucin is widely praised for its ability to hydrate, soothe irritated skin, improve pigmentation, and promote cell regeneration, making it an effective ingredient for all skin types.

COSRX Snail Mucin Product Recommendation

COSRX offers the Advanced Snail 96 Mucin Power Essence, which is known for delivering intense hydration and promoting healing, and the Advanced Snail 92 All-in-One Cream for a moisturizing effect.

All About Retinol

Retinol, also known as vitamin A, is one of the most effective ingredients for treating signs of aging. It works by promoting the production of collagen and speeding up cell turnover to reveal smoother, firmer skin.

Benefits of Retinol

Retinol helps reduce wrinkles, improve skin elasticity, and smooth out texture by promoting new skin cell formation. It can also help minimize acne by reducing sebum production.

COSRX Retinol Product Recommendation

COSRX offers two retinol products: the Retinol 0.1% Cream and the Retinol 0.5% Oil, providing different strengths depending on your needs.

Tips for Using Retinol

Start with lower concentrations and gradually build up. Retinol can cause irritation, so it's important to monitor your skin's reaction and pair it with hydrating serums like COSRX’s Peptide Skin Booster Serum or Hyaluronic 3 Serum.

All About Vitamin C

Vitamin C, known for its powerful antioxidant properties, is essential in preventing skin aging and promoting a healthy complexion. It fights free radicals, reduces pigmentation, and stimulates collagen production.

Benefits of Vitamin C

Vitamin C helps neutralize free radicals, brighten the skin, fade pigmentation, and promote collagen synthesis to improve skin texture and elasticity.

COSRX Vitamin C Product Recommendation

COSRX offers two highly effective Vitamin C products: the Vitamin C 13 Serum and the Vitamin C 23 Serum, formulated with pure ascorbic acid for maximum potency.

Tips for Using Vitamin C

To maintain its effectiveness, store your Vitamin C products in the refrigerator and always use sunscreen during the day when using Vitamin C in your routine.

All About Exfoliation!

If you are a COSRX user, you may know that exfoliation is important in a skincare routine. However, many people might not be familiar with the different types of chemical exfoliators or the ingredients contained in COSRX products.

Today, we will introduce chemical exfoliators and recommend COSRX's exfoliators for you.

AHA - Water-Soluble Exfoliator

AHA is a water-soluble acid often derived from sources like sugar cane, fruits, and milk. It includes components such as Glycolic acid, Lactic acid, Citric acid, Malic acid, Mandelic acid, and Tartaric acid, which are widely used for exfoliation.

AHA has a significant exfoliating effect by removing dead skin cells from the outermost layer of the skin. However, products with high concentrations can cause irritation, so care should be taken, especially for sensitive skin types.

If you're new to AHA, it’s best to start with products that contain a lower concentration of AHA and increase gradually if needed.

BHA - Oil-Soluble Exfoliator

Salicylic acid (BHA) is oil-soluble and effective at removing dead skin cells from the outer layer while also cleaning out pores and excess sebum. Like AHA, high concentrations can be toxic, so moderate use is advised.

Skincare FAQ: Toners? Toners!

You’re browsing through COSRX.com or other online beauty shops, or just walking through the aisle at your local drugstores, and you see so many products that people call “toners.” You’ve seen it, you may already be using it, and you probably heard from many people about how it’s an “essential part of their skincare routines.” But what really is a toner and why should you use it?

What are Toners?

In the old days, toners contained high concentrations of alcohol because the main purpose was to remove oil from the skin and make it less oily. The old toners often stung and dehydrated the skin by disrupting the skin’s oil and moisture balance. But hey, lots have changed over the past few years.

Nowadays, toners are water-based cosmetic products (not alcohol!) that help clarify skin and add hydration after cleansing. Different categories of toners contain various acids, glycerin, antioxidants, and/or anti-inflammatories. Since toners are water-based, they penetrate quickly into the skin, delivering hydration from the inside out. Using a cotton pad or cotton ball to apply toners can help soften the skin texture by removing dead cells from the skin’s surface. After cleansing, filling pores with hydration, and removing dead cells, your skin is ready for other skincare products like serums or moisturizers.

When should I use it?

This is an easy question to answer: Right After Cleansing! After you wash your face with a cleanser, wiping your skin with a toner-soaked cotton pad helps remove any residual impurities while evening out the skin. The ingredients in toners penetrate best when the skin is wet, prepping it for the next products applied.

How should I apply toners?

There are many ways to apply toners. You can put some on your hands and tap it directly onto your skin. Alternatively, you can use cotton pads to wipe your face, working from the center outwards. Using cotton pads is ideal for soft exfoliation and evening out skin tone. If you want to take an extra step, you can try the “7 Skin Method,” a popular Korean skincare routine in which you apply small amounts of toner in seven layers. This method is perfect for extra hydration during dry seasons.
