Summary

This section summarizes how we obtain, store, and use information about you. It is intended to provide a general overview. For complete details, please refer to the corresponding sections of this Privacy Policy.

Data Controller

Belasea

1. How we collect or obtain information about you

Personal information collected:

When registering as a member:

  • (Required) First Name, Last Name, E-mail, Password

When making a purchase:

  • Purchaser information: Name, E-mail, Country, Phone Number
  • Shipping information: Name, E-mail, Country, Address, Phone Number
  • Bank: Credit Card information, Bank Account information, Payment history, Payment password

When making a purchase as a non-member:

  • Purchaser information: Name, E-mail, Country
  • Shipping information: Name, Company (Optional), Address, City, State/Province, Zip/Postal Code, Country, Phone Number

Refund:

Bank account information for refund (Bank name, Account number, Account holder name).

Information automatically generated while using our services:

  • Service usage history, IP address, Cookies, Date of visit, Device information (OS version, Unique device identifier), ADID, IDFA, and occasionally from third parties

When participating in an event:

  • Name, Mobile phone number, Address

When selected as a winner for an event:

  • Name, Mobile phone number, Address

For customer service or dispute resolution:

  • Details of the customer service or dispute
2. How we use your information
  • Personal identification: Name, E-mail, Password, Country, Mobile phone number, Date of birth
  • Service and claim processing: Name, E-mail, Mobile phone number
  • Purchase and shipping: Purchase and shipping information (Name, E-mail, Mobile phone number, Address), Bank information (Credit card, Bank account, Payment history, Payment password)
  • Notifications for events and services: Name, E-mail, Mobile phone number, Address, Date of Birth, Gender, Cookies, ADID, IDFA
  • Refunds: Bank account details for refunds
  • Fraud prevention, customer dispute resolution, and customer service

We also use your information for business administration, order processing, improving our website, fulfilling contracts, advertising, analyzing usage, and complying with legal obligations.

Your information is shared only to the extent necessary, including with service providers, and in compliance with legal obligations. We do not sell your information to third parties.

3. How long we retain your information

We retain your information for no longer than necessary, considering legal obligations (e.g., tax compliance) and other legal bases (e.g., consent or performance of a contract).

The following internal policies apply:

  • To prevent loss due to abnormal membership termination: 5 days after termination request
  • To prevent unlawful gains or identity theft: Name, E-mail, and related information for 6 months after membership termination

Information retained under applicable laws includes customer disputes, financial transactions, and tax records for specified periods.

4. How we secure your information

We employ appropriate technical and organizational measures, such as secure servers, SSL encryption, and access restrictions for trusted personnel trained in personal information handling.

5. How we process and destroy your personal information

Personal information is destroyed without delay once the purpose of collection is fulfilled. Inactive accounts are handled according to applicable laws and internal policies.

6. Providing Personal Information to Third Parties

The Company does not provide personal information to third parties beyond the original purpose of collection. User consent will be obtained if necessary.

7. Outsourcing of Processing of Personal Information

The Company may outsource tasks to service providers, ensuring compliance with personal information protection laws.

8. Linked Websites

We provide links to third-party websites but are not responsible for their content or privacy policies.

9. Rights of Users and Exercise of Rights

Users can view, modify, or request the termination of their account and personal information at any time.

10. Responsibilities of Members

Users are responsible for protecting their personal information and ensuring accuracy in the provided details.

11. Cookies and Automatic Personal Information Collection

We use cookies to improve user experience. Users may accept or refuse cookies through browser settings.

12. Technological/Managerial Safeguards for Personal Information

We implement measures to protect personal information from theft, alteration, leakage, or other threats.

Final Note

For further assistance regarding personal information, please contact the relevant authorities listed in the original policy. Any changes to this Privacy Policy will be communicated in advance.
