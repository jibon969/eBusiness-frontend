Shipping Policy

Received a defective item or a product you did not order?

If you have received a wrong or defective item, please contact our Customer Support Team as soon as possible. We will investigate the issue and get back to you with a solution. Please make sure to enter your contact information and photo(s) of the products you received and the parcel box for better and faster assistance.

IMPORTANT:

Since we are shipping products from fulfillment centers based in the United States, shipping to Canada may be subject to tax and duties as it is considered a cross-border international shipment. Please be aware that extra customs fees may be imposed for shipments to Canada; we recommend that you check the estimated tax duties prior to placing orders on BelaCosmetics.com. Please check the FAQ page.

The recipient of the order will be responsible for any import duties or taxes charged by the recipient's country as part of clearing their parcel through customs. We will not be accepting returns on orders due to taxes issues.

1. International Shipping

We offer international shipping to the following countries:

  • Shipping Available: Within the Domestic U.S., Canada
  • Shipping Not Available: Outside the U.S, Puerto Rico, U.S Virgin Islands, Armed Forces Americas, and Yukon territory in Canada.
  • Note: We cannot ship to P.O. boxes.

2. Delivery Failure

If your order has been returned to us due to an invalid address or multiple failed attempts to deliver, we will refund the original order price excluding shipping costs. Please ensure all shipping details are correct at checkout.
